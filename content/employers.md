### [CARD ROW] — The Adaptive Care Model

**The right care, the moment it's needed — and it adapts as needs change**

_Lead:_ Every employee is matched to the right level and type of support based on what they actually need — not a one-size-fits-all path to therapy.

Every employee is routed to the right support — coaching, therapy, Circles peer groups, or Pathways self-guided programs — and their care adapts as their needs change, without re-enrollment or starting over.

Around 20% of your workforce engages with Modern Health, compared with the typical 2–4% for EAPs — driven by the breadth of support and a launch partnership built to drive adoption.
