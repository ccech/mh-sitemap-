### [ACCORDION FAQ] — What HR leaders ask about the member experience

**Q: What experience do employees actually get with Modern Health?**
A single place for the whole spectrum of support — coaching and self-guided Pathways for everyday wellbeing, Circles for community, and therapy for clinical needs — matched to each person and adapting over time.

**Q: What kinds of support can your people access — coaching, therapy, community, self-guided tools?**
All of them, in one platform: 1:1 coaching, licensed therapy, facilitator-led Circles, and self-guided Pathways — so every employee finds a way in that fits.

**Q: Will employees actually use it — and is it private enough that they trust it?**
Yes — engagement reaches around 20% (vs. 2–4% for EAPs), driven by breadth and by privacy: an employee's usage is never visible to their employer, only anonymous, aggregate data.

**Q: How quickly and easily can employees get started, and in what languages?**
Employees sign up with a work email, answer a few questions, and get matched — often the same day, with support in 80+ languages.

**Q: How does support adapt to each employee's needs over time?**
The Adaptive Care Model moves each person between coaching, therapy, Circles, and Pathways as their needs change — no re-enrollment, no starting over.

> _Why:_ Answers all five SEO-brief key questions verbatim (now employer-voiced). Reinforces engagement + privacy + breadth — the member-experience story an employer is evaluating.
