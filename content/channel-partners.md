### [ACCORDION FAQ] — What partners ask before we start

**Q: What types of partnerships does Modern Health support?**
PEOs and benefit administrators (embed in your standard package), brokers and consultants (add to your recommendation portfolio), benefits platforms (API/SSO integration), and co-sell/resell partners (shared commercial model). The structure adapts to how you work — not the other way around.

**Q: How does the channel partner model work commercially?**
Partnerships are structured around your distribution model — co-sell revenue sharing, embedded pricing within existing packages, or reseller arrangements. Contract terms, pricing mechanics, and white-label options are discussed with our partnerships team; no one-size model.

**Q: What resources and support do partners receive?**
RFP documentation, clinical outcomes data, co-branded materials, partner webinars, and sales enablement training. A dedicated partner success contact. Implementation and activation support for every employer client you bring on.

> _Why:_ All three SEO brief keyQuestions answered verbatim. Reinforces flexibility (partner types), commercial transparency (no fixed model), and partner enablement depth.
